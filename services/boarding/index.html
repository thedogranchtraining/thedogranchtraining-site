Regular Boarding

If your dog has not completed one of our Board & Train programs but would still like to stay at The Dog Ranch, our Regular Boarding option is available.

While boarding with us, your dog will be cared for by Juliann and her family, and treated as one of our own. Dogs receive plenty of attention and have opportunities to socialize and play with other socially appropriate dogs who may be boarding during the same time.

All boarding dogs stay inside the main family home in a climate-controlled, kennel-style area, ensuring safety and comfort throughout their stay. We work with your schedule and the availability of our space to accommodate your needs whenever possible.

Pricing: $65 per night

To check availability or ask questions, call or text (443) 935-0691 or email TheDogRanchTraining@gmail.com.
