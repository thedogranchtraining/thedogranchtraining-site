Regular Boarding

If your dog has not completed one of our Board & Train programs but you would still like to board at The Dog Ranch, Regular Boarding is a great option. While boarding at The Dog Ranch, your dog will be cared for by the Elmco family and loved on as our own.

Your dog will not only receive the attention they need, but will also have the opportunity to socialize and play with other socially appropriate dogs that may be boarding during the same time as your own.

Dogs stay inside the main Elmco house, in a kennel-like area that is climate controlled to ensure safety and comfort.

We will work with you, your schedule, and the availability of our space.

Pricing: $65 per night

Spa Specials

If your dog signs up for Regular Boarding, you can add on any of the Spa Specials below:

  • Bath — $15
  • De-shedding — $10
  • Nail trimming — $10

Please let The Dog Ranch staff know how you would like to customize your dog’s spa package.
