Board & Train

In our Board & Train programs, your dog will stay at The Dog Ranch, where they will be taught and cared for by the owner and founder, Juliann. Juliann is a certified trainer specializing in dog obedience and behavior modification.

During their stay, dogs are cared for not only by Juliann, but also by her family, and are treated and loved as if they were our own.

Board & Train is an excellent option for many dogs because it provides a stable, low-stress learning environment, which is crucial for building new behaviors and establishing a strong foundation of obedience.

Board & Train inquiries can be made by calling or texting (443) 935-0691, or by emailing TheDogRanchTraining@gmail.com.

Things to Know

While staying at The Dog Ranch, your dog will receive focused training and individualized attention, while also having opportunities to socialize and play with other socially appropriate dogs.

All Board & Train dogs stay inside the family home in a climate-controlled, kennel-style area, ensuring both safety and comfort. There is no specific enrollment timeline — we work with your schedule and the availability of our space.

Requirements

All Board & Train programs require owners to provide:

  • Enough food to last the duration of the stay
  • A valid Rabies certificate
  • A current copy of vaccinations
  • Any medications your dog may need

A $100 non-refundable deposit is required to hold a Board & Train space.

The Foundation

All Board & Train packages include the following FOUNDATION skills:

  • Initial Consultation
  • Manners – addressing common issues such as leash pulling, jumping on guests, barking, and bolting through doors
  • Heeling – your dog will heel at your side and automatically sit when you stop
  • Sit / Down
  • Down Stay – remaining in a down position at a designated location of your choice
  • Place – remaining at a designated place you select
  • Private Graduation Handover Session

PLUS:
✔️ Three (3) FREE follow-up sessions at The Dog Ranch, usable within one year of graduation

Behavior Modification

If your dog is experiencing behavioral challenges, we can help. We offer customized training programs designed to address specific behavioral concerns through a structured and individualized approach.

After an initial consultation, you’ll work directly with Head Trainer Juliann to determine the most appropriate course of action for your dog. Behavior Modification programs are available within the 4- or 6-week Board & Train options.

Common behavioral concerns we work with include:

  • Aggression toward other dogs
  • History of biting
  • Resource guarding
  • Shyness
  • Anxiety

Contact us to discuss pricing and determine whether this option is the best fit for your dog.
