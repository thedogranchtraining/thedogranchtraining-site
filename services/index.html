Our Services

At The Dog Ranch, we offer thoughtfully designed training and boarding options to support dogs and their owners at every stage. Each service is tailored to provide structure, consistency, and care in a safe and supportive environment.

Board & Train

Our Board & Train programs offer an immersive training experience where dogs stay at The Ranch and receive focused, hands-on training. Programs are designed to build reliable obedience, improve manners, and establish a strong foundation for long-term success, all in a structured and low-stress environment.

View Details

Behavioral Consultation

Is your dog displaying unwanted or challenging behaviors? We offer behavioral consultations to help identify the underlying causes of these behaviors and develop a thoughtful, individualized plan to address them.

Behavioral consultations are typically incorporated into our Board & Train programs following an initial evaluation.

View Details

Refresher Options

Even well-trained dogs benefit from occasional practice and reinforcement. Our Refresher Options are available for dogs who have previously completed a Board & Train program with us.

Options include:

  • Refresher Week – A focused week of dedicated training to reinforce and strengthen previously learned commands
  • Boarding with Maintenance – A boarding option that includes continued reinforcement of trained commands and manners
View Details

Regular Boarding

Planning a trip? Our Regular Boarding option provides safe, comfortable overnight care for dogs who have not completed a Board & Train program.

Dogs stay in a calm, supervised environment and receive plenty of attention, care, and social interaction while you’re away.

Spa Specials

We also offer Spa Specials as optional add-ons to select services. These services help ensure your dog returns home clean, comfortable, and refreshed following their stay at The Dog Ranch.
