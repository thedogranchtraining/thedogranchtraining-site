Refresher Options

After your dog has completed one of our Board & Train programs, they are eligible for our Refresher Options. These programs are designed to reinforce previously learned skills and help get your dog back on track.

Boarding with Maintenance

Once your dog has completed a Board & Train program with The Dog Ranch, they may qualify for our Boarding with Maintenance option. This is a great alternative to standard boarding.

Your dog will stay with people they already know and trust while receiving maintenance on commands and manners learned during their training.

Price: Discounted compared to Regular Boarding

Refresher Week

After completing one of our training programs, your dog is eligible for a Refresher Week. This is a great option if you have been unable to maintain your dog’s training at home.

During this week, we focus on retraining and reinforcing all previously learned skills. This option differs from Boarding with Maintenance in that it includes dedicated, structured training time each day.

A handover session is included at pickup to review the skills your dog has relearned and to provide guidance for maintaining them at home.

Email us for pricing.
