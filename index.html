Welcome to The Dog Ranch

The Dog Ranch is an in-home dog training and boarding business located in the Hereford Zone of Northern Baltimore County. We provide a safe, structured, and loving environment for dogs to learn, grow, and feel at home.

About Us

Founded by Juliann Elmco, The Dog Ranch was built on a lifelong love of dogs and a passion for helping owners and their dogs build strong, healthy relationships.
